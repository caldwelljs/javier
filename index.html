Javier Gonzalez, born in El Paso, Texas, and educated in the United States, Mexico and Switzerland, entered the film business after graduating from Instituto Tecnologico Autonomo de Mexico Law School in 2003. In 2004 Javier attended the New York Film Academy and has since worked in various production capacities including feature films, TV shows, music videos, short films and commercials in the United States, Mexico and Argentina.

Javier is fluent in English, Spanish and German and currently resides in Brooklyn, New York.
